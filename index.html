Youth Leadership Summit.

August 6th, 9:00 AM to 4:30 PM, at the Cupertino Quinlan Center. Free for youth; invitation-only for adults.

You can make a difference.

High schoolers, college students, and young professionals are invited to a day of learning, networking, and discussion around solutions and strategies to combat climate change, hosted by Silicon Valley Youth Climate Action!

This summit will include workshops, keynote speakers, and panel presentations that empower attendees to get further involved in the environmental sustainability movement.

Join us for a day of interactive workshops as well as informative speaker programs that stretch across a vast array of urgent environmental topics, such as state legislation, green career paths, environmental literacy, and effective climate action.

Breakfast and lunch will be provided.

Hear from our featured speakers.

  • Peggy Brannigan, Director of Global Environmental Sustainability at LinkedIn
  • Dave Cortese, California State Senator for District 15
  • Jan Pepper, CEO Peninsula Clean Energy
  • Laura Deehan, State Director for Environment California
  • Jasneet Sharma, Director of the Santa Clara County Office of Sustainability
  • Dr. Mary Ann Dewan, Santa Clara County Superintendent of Schools
  • David Cohen, San Jose City Councilmember
  • Ten Strands
  • Silicon Valley Youth Climate Activists
  • ...And MANY more special guests!
